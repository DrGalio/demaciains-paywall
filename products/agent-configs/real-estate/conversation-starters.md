# Real Estate Agent AI — Conversation Starters

These 10 prompts are ready to use as suggested conversation starters in your ChatGPT Custom GPT, Claude Project, or Gemini Gems setup. They cover the most common and high-value interactions a real estate AI assistant should handle.

---

## 1. First-Time Homebuyer Walkthrough

**"I'm thinking about buying my first home but I have no idea where to start. Can you walk me through the entire process from start to finish?"**

*Purpose: Educates nervous first-time buyers and builds trust by demonstrating expertise. Covers pre-approval, home search, offers, inspections, and closing in a clear, non-overwhelming way.*

---

## 2. Home Valuation Estimate

**"I'm considering selling my home. How can I figure out what it's worth, and what factors affect the price?"**

*Purpose: Opens a listing consultation conversation. Explains CMAs, comparable sales, and market conditions. Positions the agent as knowledgeable and data-driven.*

---

## 3. Buy vs. Rent Analysis

**"I'm currently renting. How do I know if it's the right time to buy instead?"**

*Purpose: Addresses a common hesitation point. Walks through financial comparison, equity building, lifestyle factors, and market timing. Helps nurture leads who are on the fence.*

---

## 4. Selling Strategy Planning

**"I need to sell my home in the next 3-4 months. What should I be doing right now to get ready?"**

*Purpose: Creates a pre-listing action plan covering staging, repairs, pricing strategy, timing, and marketing. Demonstrates the AI's ability to think strategically and build timelines.*

---

## 5. Investment Property Evaluation

**"I'm looking at buying a rental property. How do I figure out if it's a good investment?"**

*Purpose: Speaks to investor clients with data-driven analysis. Covers cap rates, cash flow, vacancy assumptions, and ROI calculations. Shows the AI's analytical depth.*

---

## 6. Mortgage Options Comparison

**"What are the differences between FHA, conventional, and VA loans, and which one might be best for me?"**

*Purpose: Helps buyers understand their financing options. Explains eligibility, down payment requirements, PMI, and pros/cons of each loan type. Recommends consulting a lender for specifics.*

---

## 7. Neighborhood Deep Dive

**"I'm relocating to [city name]. What should I know about the different neighborhoods and where should I look?"**

*Purpose: Engages relocation clients who need comprehensive area information. Guides them through research methodology, key decision factors, and how to evaluate neighborhoods remotely.*

---

## 8. Offer Negotiation Coaching

**"I found a home I love, but it's priced above my budget. How should I approach making an offer?"**

*Purpose: Provides strategic negotiation guidance. Covers offer structure, contingencies, escalation clauses, and how to present a compelling offer below asking price without being insulting.*

---

## 9. Market Timing Question

**"Everyone says the market is about to crash / interest rates are going up / prices are dropping. Should I wait to buy?"**

*Purpose: Addresses common market anxiety with balanced perspective. Explains how market timing is less important than personal financial readiness, and discusses historical context for market fears.*

---

## 10. Post-Inspection Negotiation

**"The inspection found some issues with the home I'm under contract on. How do I handle this with the seller?"**

*Purpose: Guides buyers through a stressful but common situation. Explains what issues are worth negotiating versus accepting, how to request repairs or credits, and when to walk away.*
