# Dental Practice AI — Conversation Starters

10 ready-to-use conversation starters for your ChatGPT Custom GPT, Claude Project, or Gemini Gem. Each covers high-value dental patient interactions.

---

## 1. New Patient Welcome & Scheduling

**"Hi, I'm new to the area and looking for a dentist. What should I know about your practice and how do I schedule my first appointment?"**

*Purpose: First impression conversation that welcomes new patients, introduces the practice, and streamlines booking. Covers what to bring, what to expect, and builds trust.*

---

## 2. Emergency Dental Triage

**"I have a really bad toothache that started last night. What should I do? Is this an emergency?"**

*Purpose: Provides immediate triage guidance — pain management, urgency assessment, when to seek care. Reduces anxiety and prevents unnecessary ER visits. Positions the practice as accessible and caring.*

---

## 3. Treatment Cost Estimate

**"My dentist said I need a crown. How much does that cost, and will my insurance cover it?"**

*Purpose: Addresses cost anxiety transparently. Explains procedure costs, insurance coverage percentages, and payment options. Converts price-shoppers into booked appointments by demystifying dental economics.*

---

## 4. Teeth Whitening Consultation

**"I want whiter teeth for my wedding in 3 months. What are my options and how long will results last?"**

*Purpose: Opens cosmetic dentistry conversation with urgency and motivation. Compares in-office vs. take-home options, sets realistic expectations, and helps patients choose the right treatment.*

---

## 5. Dental Anxiety Support

**"I haven't been to the dentist in 5 years because I'm terrified. Can you help me understand what to expect?"**

*Purpose: Addresses dental phobia with empathy and concrete information. Explains comfort options (sedation, headphones, breaks), normalizes their feelings, and makes the first step feel achievable.*

---

## 6. Insurance Benefits Check

**"I have [Insurance Name]. What's covered and what will I owe out of pocket?"**

*Purpose: Helps patients understand their benefits before they visit. Reduces billing surprises, builds trust, and ensures patients feel informed. Explains preventive/basic/major coverage tiers.*

---

## 7. Post-Procedure Recovery Guide

**"I just had a tooth extracted. What can I eat, what should I avoid, and how do I know if something's wrong?"**

*Purpose: Post-procedure support that reduces after-hours calls. Covers diet, pain management, warning signs, and when to call the office. Patients feel cared for even after leaving.*

---

## 8. Invisalign / Orthodontic Inquiry

**"Am I too old for braces? I'm 35 and my teeth have shifted. What are my options?"**

*Purpose: Opens adult orthodontics conversation. Explains Invisalign benefits, treatment timeline, cost, and lifestyle impact. Overcomes "braces are for kids" mental barrier.*

---

## 9. Kids' First Dental Visit

**"My child is 2 years old and has never seen a dentist. When should they start and what happens at the first visit?"**

*Purpose: Guides parents through pediatric dental milestones. Explains first-visit expectations, makes it feel safe and fun, and positions the practice as family-friendly.*

---

## 10. Dental Implant Education

**"I'm missing a tooth and considering an implant vs. a bridge. What are the pros and cons?"**

*Purpose: High-value consultation that educates on implant vs. bridge vs. partial denture options. Covers longevity, cost, procedure steps, and candidacy requirements. Positions the dentist as the expert worth investing in.*
