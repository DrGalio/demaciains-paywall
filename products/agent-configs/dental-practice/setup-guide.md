# Dental Practice AI Agent — Setup Guide

Get your AI dental assistant running in under 15 minutes. Works with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

---

## What's Included in This Pack

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `system-prompt.md` | The AI's personality, knowledge, and behavior rules |
| `knowledge-base.md` | Customizable practice info template (fill in your details) |
| `conversation-starters.md` | 10 ready-to-use patient interaction prompts |
| `setup-guide.md` | This file — step-by-step setup instructions |

---

## Option A: ChatGPT Custom GPT (Recommended)

1. Go to **chat.openai.com** → Click your profile → **My GPTs** → **Create a GPT**
2. In the **Instructions** field, paste the entire contents of `system-prompt.md`
3. Below the system prompt, add: `## Practice Knowledge Base` and paste your customized `knowledge-base.md`
4. In the **Conversation Starters** section, paste the 10 starters from `conversation-starters.md`
5. Set a name: e.g., "Dr. Smith's Dental Assistant"
6. Set a description: "Your dental practice AI assistant — scheduling, treatment questions, insurance help, and more."
7. Click **Save** → **Publish** (private to your team or public)
8. Share the link with your staff

**Pro tip:** In GPT Builder, upload your practice's PDF brochure or FAQ document as additional knowledge.

---

## Option B: Claude Project

1. Go to **claude.ai** → **Projects** → **Create Project**
2. Name it: "Dental Practice Assistant"
3. In **Project Instructions**, paste the system prompt + knowledge base (combined)
4. Upload any additional docs (price lists, insurance summaries) to the project
5. Start a conversation and add conversation starters as needed
6. Share the project link with your team

---

## Option C: Gemini Gems

1. Go to **gemini.google.com** → **Gems** → **Create a Gem**
2. Name: "Dental Office AI"
3. In **Instructions**, paste the system prompt
4. Add the knowledge base as a separate section
5. Save and share with your Google Workspace team

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific in your knowledge base.** The more real data you provide, the better the AI performs.
2. **Test edge cases.** Ask the AI about procedures you don't offer, insurance you don't accept, etc.
3. **Update regularly.** When hours, pricing, or staff change, update the knowledge base.
4. **Set boundaries.** The AI should never diagnose or prescribe — only educate and guide.
5. **Monitor conversations.** Review AI interactions weekly to catch and correct any issues.
6. **Combine with human touch.** Use the AI for routine inquiries; keep complex cases for staff.
