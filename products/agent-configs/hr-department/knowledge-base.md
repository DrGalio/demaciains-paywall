## Q10: How do I request an accommodation (disability, religious, or otherwise)?

**A:** We are committed to providing reasonable accommodations so every employee can perform their job effectively. Here's how the process works:

**Types of accommodations:**
- **Disability accommodations:** Ergonomic equipment, assistive technology, modified schedules, remote work, job restructuring, accessible parking
- **Religious accommodations:** Schedule adjustments for religious observances, prayer space, dietary accommodations at company events, dress code exceptions
- **Pregnancy/nursing accommodations:** Modified duties, flexible scheduling, lactation room access
- **Other accommodations:** Situations where a temporary or permanent adjustment would help you perform your role

**The process:**
1. **Initiate:** Contact [HR CONTACT] to request an accommodation. You can also speak with your manager, who will connect you with HR.
2. **Interactive process:** HR will work with you and your manager to understand your needs and identify effective accommodations. This is a collaborative conversation — your input is essential.
3. **Documentation:** For disability accommodations, medical documentation may be requested to understand the nature of the limitation (not the diagnosis). Your medical information is kept confidential and shared only with those who need to know.
4. **Implementation:** Once agreed upon, accommodations are implemented promptly. A timeline is established for each accommodation.
5. **Review:** Accommodations are reviewed periodically to ensure they remain effective. You can request adjustments at any time.

**Your rights:**
- You are not required to disclose a specific diagnosis — only the functional limitation
- All accommodation discussions are confidential
- Denial of a reasonable accommodation can be appealed through [HR LEAD / ETHICS CONTACT]
- Retaliation for requesting an accommodation is prohibited

**Common accommodations we provide:**
- Standing desks, ergonomic chairs, and keyboard/mouse setups
- Screen readers and magnification software
- Flexible start/end times for medical appointments
- Remote work arrangements
- Modified break schedules for medical needs
- Service animal approval
- Noise-canceling headphones in open office environments

Contact [HR CONTACT] to start the conversation. We're here to find solutions that work for you and the organization.
