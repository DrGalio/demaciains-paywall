# HR Department AI — Conversation Starters

These 10 prompts are ready to use as suggested conversation starters in your ChatGPT Custom GPT, Claude Project, or Gemini Gems setup. They cover the most common and high-value interactions an HR AI assistant should handle.

---

## 1. New Hire Onboarding

**"I just started and I'm not sure what I need to do this first week. Can you walk me through everything?"**

*Purpose: Eases first-day anxiety. Walks the new hire through pre-boarding tasks, Day 1 logistics, benefits enrollment deadlines, mandatory training, and who to contact for what. Sets a welcoming, supportive tone from the start.*

---

## 2. Benefits Enrollment

**"Open enrollment is coming up and I'm overwhelmed by all the plan options. Can you help me compare them?"**

*Purpose: Guides employees through health, dental, vision, FSA/HSA, and supplemental benefit options. Asks about their situation (family size, expected medical needs, budget) and recommends appropriate plans. Simplifies a complex decision.*

---

## 3. Leave Request

**"I need to take some time off for [a family emergency / medical leave / personal reasons]. What are my options?"**

*Purpose: Identifies the right type of leave (PTO, sick leave, FMLA, personal leave, bereavement), explains eligibility and process, and guides the employee through the next steps. Handles sensitive situations with empathy.*

---

## 4. Policy Clarification

**"I'm not sure I understand our [remote work / dress code / social media / attendance] policy. Can you explain it to me?"**

*Purpose: Provides clear, plain-language explanations of company policies. Avoids legalistic jargon. Helps employees understand not just what the policy says, but why it exists and how it applies to their situation.*

---

## 5. Performance Review Prep

**"My annual review is next month. How should I prepare? What makes a strong self-assessment?"**

*Purpose: Coaches employees through the review process. Covers self-assessment tips (STAR method, quantifying impact, honest self-reflection), goal-setting frameworks, and how to have a productive review conversation with their manager.*

---

## 6. Workplace Concern

**"Something happened at work that's making me uncomfortable. I'm not sure if it's worth reporting or who to talk to."**

*Purpose: Creates a safe space for employees to share concerns. Explains reporting options (manager, HR, anonymous hotline), emphasizes confidentiality and anti-retaliation protections, and helps the employee understand what happens after a report. Takes every concern seriously.*

---

## 7. DEI Question

**"I'd like to learn more about our DEI programs. What Employee Resource Groups can I join, and how can I get more involved?"**

*Purpose: Introduces available ERGs, DEI initiatives, cultural events, mentoring programs, and volunteer opportunities. Encourages participation regardless of identity. Explains how to join, start an ERG, or contribute to DEI efforts.*

---

## 8. Payroll Inquiry

**"I noticed something on my paycheck that doesn't look right. Can you help me understand my pay stub?"**

*Purpose: Walks employees through pay stub components (gross pay, deductions, withholdings, pre-tax/post-tax items, overtime, bonuses). Helps identify potential discrepancies and directs them to payroll for corrections. Explains direct deposit and W-4 updates.*

---

## 9. Career Development

**"I want to grow within the company but I'm not sure what paths are available or how to get started."**

*Purpose: Explores career development resources — internal job postings, tuition reimbursement, mentorship programs, leadership development, and skill-building opportunities. Helps employees map a growth trajectory and create an actionable development plan.*

---

## 10. Exit Process

**"I've decided to move on from the company. What do I need to do to resign properly and make sure the transition goes smoothly?"**

*Purpose: Guides departing employees through the resignation process — notice period, resignation letter, transition planning, benefits continuation (COBRA), final paycheck, PTO payout, company property return, and exit interview. Handles the conversation with professionalism and respect.*
