# Restaurant AI — Conversation Starters

These 10 prompts are ready to use as suggested conversation starters in your ChatGPT Custom GPT, Claude Project, or Gemini Gems setup. They cover the most common and high-value interactions a restaurant AI assistant should handle.

---

## 1. New Guest Welcome

**"Hi! I've never been to your restaurant before. What should I know before visiting?"**

*Purpose: Creates a warm first impression. Gives the AI a chance to introduce the restaurant's cuisine, ambiance, signature dishes, hours, and what makes the dining experience special. Sets the tone for a personalized relationship.*

---

## 2. Reservation Booking

**"I'd like to make a reservation for [number] people on [date] at [time]. Can you help me with that?"**

*Purpose: The most common interaction. Guides the guest through booking details, asks about special occasions, dietary needs, and seating preferences. Demonstrates the AI's ability to handle logistics smoothly.*

---

## 3. Dietary Restrictions Inquiry

**"I'm [vegan / gluten-free / allergic to shellfish]. What can I eat from your menu?"**

*Purpose: Addresses a common and sometimes anxiety-inducing concern. Shows the AI's ability to parse dietary needs, reference the allergen matrix, and recommend specific dishes with confidence and empathy.*

---

## 4. Private Dining Exploration

**"I'm planning a dinner for [number] guests — do you have a private space available? What are the options?"**

*Purpose: Opens a high-value private event inquiry. Walks the guest through room options, capacity, prix fixe menus, customization options, and pricing. Positions the restaurant as event-ready.*

---

## 5. Takeout Order

**"I'd like to place a takeout order. What travels well and what do you recommend?"**

*Purpose: Helps guests choose dishes that hold up during transport. Shows the AI's knowledge of food quality considerations and upsells strategically — suggesting appetizers, desserts, or beverages.*

---

## 6. Wine & Beverage Pairing

**"We're ordering [dish names]. What wine or cocktail would you pair with that?"**

*Purpose: Showcases the restaurant's beverage program and the AI's sommelier-level knowledge. Creates a curated, elevated experience. Can also guide non-drinkers toward mocktails or non-alcoholic options.*

---

## 7. Birthday or Anniversary Celebration

**"It's my [birthday / anniversary] — can you help make it extra special?"**

*Purpose: Triggers a celebration workflow. Offers custom cakes, champagne, special seating, personalized menus, and any other touches the restaurant provides. Creates a memorable experience that drives word-of-mouth.*

---

## 8. Allergy Safety Concern

**"My child has a severe [allergy type]. How do you handle cross-contamination in the kitchen?"**

*Purpose: Addresses a high-stakes, emotionally charged situation. Demonstrates the AI's ability to be thorough, empathetic, and transparent about safety protocols — while recommending direct communication with the kitchen manager for severe cases.*

---

## 9. Group or Corporate Booking

**"We're a team of [number] looking for a spot for our company dinner. What can you offer?"**

*Purpose: Opens a corporate/group inquiry. Covers private dining, prix fixe menus, AV capabilities, dietary accommodations for large groups, and billing options. Positions the restaurant as business-friendly.*

---

## 10. Post-Visit Feedback

**"I dined with you last night and [loved it / had an issue]. How can I share my feedback?"**

*Purpose: Handles both positive and negative feedback gracefully. For positive experiences, thanks the guest and invites them to leave a review. For negative experiences, expresses genuine concern, gathers details, and offers to connect with a manager. Shows the AI's emotional intelligence.*
