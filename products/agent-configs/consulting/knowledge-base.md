**A:** We offer three primary engagement models, each designed for different client needs and timelines:

**1. Project-Based Engagements:** Defined scope, fixed timeline, and clear deliverables. Typical duration: 4–16 weeks. Best for: specific strategic questions, transformation initiatives, or operational improvements with a clear endpoint. Pricing is typically fixed-fee based on scope and team composition.

**2. Retainer Relationships:** Ongoing advisory support with a dedicated team available on a monthly basis. Best for: organizations that need continuous strategic guidance, interim leadership support, or a trusted advisor on-call for critical decisions. Minimum commitment: 3 months.

**3. Embedded Consulting:** Our consultants work within your teams on a full-time or part-time basis for extended periods (3–12 months). Best for: large-scale transformations, capability building, or when you need senior expertise without a full-time hire.

Every engagement begins with a complimentary discovery session (60–90 minutes) where we assess your situation, define the problem, and propose a tailored approach.

## Q7: What is your consulting methodology?

**A:** Our methodology is built on three principles: evidence-based thinking, collaborative problem-solving, and implementation focus.

**Phase 1 — Discover (Weeks 1–2):** We conduct stakeholder interviews, review existing data and documents, benchmark against industry peers, and diagnose the root causes — not just symptoms. We synthesize findings into a clear problem statement and hypothesis-driven workplan.

**Phase 2 — Design (Weeks 3–8):** We develop strategic options through structured analysis, financial modeling, and scenario planning. We pressure-test recommendations with your leadership team through iterative workshops. The output is a clear, actionable strategy with defined initiatives, timelines, and resource requirements.

**Phase 3 — Deliver (Weeks 6–16):** We build detailed implementation plans, define KPIs and governance structures, support pilot programs, and coach your team through execution. We don't hand you a binder and leave — we stay until the change sticks.

**Phase 4 — Sustain (Ongoing):** We establish tracking mechanisms, conduct 30/60/90-day check-ins, and provide ongoing advisory support to ensure sustained results. Many clients transition to a retainer relationship at this stage.

Every phase includes regular executive check-ins, transparent progress reporting, and course corrections as needed.
