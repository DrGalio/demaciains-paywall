# Conversation Starters: E-Commerce AI Shopping Assistant

> These conversation starters are designed to be used as welcome messages, proactive prompts, or quick-action buttons in your AI shopping assistant interface. Customize the language to match your brand voice.

---

## 1. 👋 New Customer Welcome & Browsing Help

**Trigger:** First-time visitor or new customer initiates chat

**Starter:**
> "Welcome to [STORE NAME]! 🛍️ I'm your personal shopping assistant, here to help you find exactly what you're looking for. Whether you need product recommendations, sizing help, or just want to browse our latest arrivals — I've got you covered. What brings you in today?"

**Follow-up options:**
- "Browse our newest arrivals"
- "I'm looking for something specific"
- "Show me today's deals"
- "Help me find a gift"

**Guidelines:**
- Keep the welcome warm but not overwhelming
- Offer 3-4 clear paths forward
- Mention current promotions if applicable
- Don't assume purchase intent — some visitors are just browsing

---

## 2. 📦 Order Status Check

**Trigger:** Returning customer or explicit order inquiry

**Starter:**
> "Hi! I'd be happy to help you check on your order. Could you share your order number or the email address associated with your account? I'll pull up the details right away."

**If order number provided:**
> "Found it! Here's the status of order #[ORDER NUMBER]:
> 📋 **Status:** [Shipped / Processing / Delivered]
> 🚚 **Tracking:** [TRACKING NUMBER]
> 📅 **Estimated Delivery:** [DATE]
>
> Is there anything else you'd like to know about this order?"

**Alternative (account holder):**
> "I see you have a recent order — let me pull up the latest status for you."

**Guidelines:**
- Always ask for order number or email for verification
- Provide clear, scannable status updates
- Proactively mention tracking links
- If delayed, acknowledge and explain before being asked

## 3. 📏 Size & Fit Recommendation

**Trigger:** Customer asks about sizing, or is viewing a product page

**Starter:**
> "I'd love to help you find the perfect fit! To give you the best recommendation:
>
> 1. What size do you typically wear in [similar brand/item type]?
> 2. Do you prefer a fitted or relaxed look?
> 3. Are you between sizes?
>
> I'll cross-reference with our size chart and customer feedback to find your ideal match."

**Follow-up example:**
> "Based on what you've told me, I'd recommend a **Medium** in this item. Here's why:
> - This brand runs slightly small (most customers size up)
> - Customer reviews say it fits true to size after washing
> - For a relaxed fit, some customers prefer Large
>
> Would you like me to check if your size is in stock?"

**Guidelines:**
- Always reference the specific product's size chart
- Mention brand-specific fit tendencies
- Use customer review data when available
- Offer to check stock immediately after making a recommendation

## 4. 🔄 Return/Exchange Request

**Trigger:** Customer mentions return, exchange, or refund

**Starter:**
> "I'm sorry to hear you'd like to return or exchange an item. Let me make this as easy as possible for you. Could you share:
>
> 1. Your order number
> 2. Which item(s) you'd like to return or exchange
> 3. The reason (too small, didn't like it, defective, etc.)
>
> I'll take it from there!"

**Return flow:**
> "I've processed your return request:
> ✅ **Return label:** Sent to [EMAIL]
> 📦 **Instructions:** Pack item with tags attached, drop at any [CARRIER] location
> 💰 **Refund:** $[AMOUNT] to your original payment method (3-5 business days after we receive it)
>
> Is there anything else I can help with?"

**Exchange flow:**
> "Great news — the [NEW SIZE/COLOR] you want is in stock! Here's the plan:
> 1. I'll ship your new item today
> 2. You'll receive a return label for the original item
> 3. Drop off the original whenever it's convenient
>
> Your new item ships right away — no need to wait for the return. Sound good?"

**Guidelines:**
- Lead with empathy
- Make the process feel effortless
- Always offer exchange before processing return
- If item is outside return window, explain policy gently and offer alternatives (store credit, etc.)

## 5. ⚖️ Product Comparison

**Trigger:** Customer is deciding between multiple products

**Starter:**
> "Trying to decide between a few options? I can help! Tell me which products you're comparing, and I'll break down the differences — features, price, fit, customer reviews — so you can make the best choice."

**Comparison example:**
> "Here's a side-by-side look at your options:
>
> | Feature | Option A | Option B |
> |---------|----------|----------|
> | **Price** | $49.99 | $64.99 |
> | **Material** | Cotton blend | 100% organic cotton |
> | **Fit** | True to size | Runs slightly small |
> | **Rating** | ⭐ 4.5 (320 reviews) | ⭐ 4.8 (180 reviews) |
> | **Best for** | Everyday wear | Premium quality seekers |
>
> **My take:** If durability and eco-friendliness matter to you, Option B is worth the extra $15. If you're looking for everyday basics, Option A is excellent value.
>
> Want me to check stock in your size for either?"

**Guidelines:**
- Be objective but offer your expert opinion when asked
- Highlight key differentiators, not just specs
- Include review ratings and sentiment
- End with an actionable next step

## 6. 🛒 Cart Recovery — "You Left Items Behind"

**Trigger:** Customer has items in cart but hasn't checked out (after [X] minutes of inactivity)

**Starter:**
> "Hey! I noticed you have some great items waiting in your cart:
>
> 🛒 **[ITEM 1]** — $[PRICE] ([SIZE/COLOR])
> 🛒 **[ITEM 2]** — $[PRICE] ([SIZE/COLOR])
>
> Just wanted to make sure you didn't forget! Everything is still in stock and ready to ship. Would you like to complete your order, or do you have any questions about these items?"

**If price is a concern:**
> "I totally understand — let me check if there are any current promotions that apply to your cart..."
> "Good news! Code [CODE] gives you [X]% off. Want me to apply that?"

**If stock concern:**
> "Quick heads up — [ITEM] in your cart is down to [X] left in [SIZE]. Just want to make sure you don't miss out!"

**Guidelines:**
- Be helpful, not aggressive
- Show the items with key details (size, color, price)
- Address common hesitations (price, fit, availability)
- Never pressure — "no pressure, just wanted to check in"
- Limit cart recovery prompts to 2-3 max per session

## 7. ⭐ Loyalty Points Check

**Trigger:** Returning customer, or customer asks about rewards

**Starter:**
> "Let's check your rewards! 🎁
>
> **Current Points Balance:** [X] points
> **Tier Status:** [BRONZE/SILVER/GOLD/PLATINUM]
> **Points Value:** $[X] in rewards
>
> You're [X] points away from [NEXT TIER] — which unlocks [BENEFITS]!
>
> Want to redeem your points on your next purchase, or see how to earn more?"

**Redemption flow:**
> "You can redeem your points right at checkout — just select 'Apply Rewards' in the payment section. Here's what your points are worth:
> - 100 points = $5 off
> - 200 points = $10 off
> - 500 points = $25 off
>
> Ready to shop?"

**Earning tips:**
> "Here are some quick ways to boost your points:
> - 📝 Leave a review on a recent purchase → +[X] points
> - 👥 Refer a friend → +[X] points for both of you
> - 🎂 Update your birthday → +[X] points on your birthday month
> - 📱 Share your purchase on social → +[X] points"

**Guidelines:**
- Always show points balance and tier status prominently
- Celebrate progress toward next tier
- Make redemption feel easy and rewarding
- Suggest earning opportunities without being pushy

## 8. 🏷️ Sale & Discount Discovery

**Trigger:** Customer asks about deals, or first visit during a sale period

**Starter:**
> "Great timing! Here's what's on sale right now:
>
> 🔥 **[SALE NAME]** — [X]% off [CATEGORY/ITEMS]
> 📅 **Ends:** [DATE]
> 🏷️ **Code:** [CODE] (auto-applied at checkout)
>
> Plus, we've got free shipping on all orders over $[THRESHOLD]!
>
> Want me to show you the best deals in [CATEGORY]?"

**Personalized deals:**
> "Based on what you've bought before, I think you'd love these sale items:
> - **[ITEM 1]** — Was $[X], now $[Y] ([Z]% off)
> - **[ITEM 2]** — Was $[X], now $[Y] ([Z]% off)
> - **[ITEM 3]** — Was $[X], now $[Y] ([Z]% off)
>
> These are moving fast — just [X] left in most sizes!"

**Guidelines:**
- Lead with the best/current promotion
- Personalize recommendations based on browsing/purchase history
- Create genuine urgency (real stock levels, real end dates)
- Make it easy to shop the sale (provide categories, links)

## 9. 🎁 Gift Recommendation

**Trigger:** Customer mentions gift shopping or asks for gift ideas

**Starter:**
> "I'd love to help you find the perfect gift! Let me narrow it down:
>
> 1. **Who is it for?** (partner, parent, friend, coworker, etc.)
> 2. **What's your budget?** (under $25 / $25-50 / $50-100 / $100+)
> 3. **Any interests or style preferences?**
> 4. **Is there an occasion?** (birthday, holiday, thank you, just because)
>
> I'll curate some options tailored to your recipient!"

**Recommendation example:**
> "Here are my top picks for a [RELATIONSHIP] who loves [INTEREST]:
>
> 1. **[ITEM]** — $[PRICE]
>    *Why it's great:* [Brief, compelling reason]
>
> 2. **[ITEM]** — $[PRICE]
>    *Why it's great:* [Brief, compelling reason]
>
> 3. **[ITEM]** — $[PRICE]
>    *Why it's great:* [Brief, compelling reason]
>
> 🎁 **Pro tip:** We offer free gift wrapping and can include a personalized message!
>
> Want to add any of these to your cart?"

**Guidelines:**
- Ask qualifying questions first — don't dump a generic gift guide
- Personalize based on recipient profile (age, interests, relationship)
- Mention gift services (wrapping, gift receipts, messages)
- Suggest complementary items for gift bundles

## 10. 🚚 Shipping Estimate for Destination

**Trigger:** Customer asks about shipping costs or delivery times

**Starter:**
> "I'd be happy to get you a shipping estimate! Just need a couple of details:
>
> 1. **Your zip code or city/country**
> 2. **How many items** (or just tell me your cart total)
>
> I'll show you all available shipping options with costs and delivery dates."

**Estimate example:**
> "Here are your shipping options for [DESTINATION]:
>
> | Method | Cost | Estimated Delivery |
> |--------|------|--------------------|
> | 📦 Standard | $[X] | [DATE RANGE] |
> | 🚚 Expedited | $[X] | [DATE RANGE] |
> | ✈️ Express | $[X] | [DATE RANGE] |
>
> 💡 **Good news!** Your cart qualifies for **free standard shipping** (orders over $[THRESHOLD]).
>
> Orders placed before [TIME] [TIMEZONE] ship today!"

**International shipping:**
> "For international orders to [COUNTRY], please note:
> - Customs duties and taxes may apply at delivery (not included in our shipping cost)
> - International deliveries typically take [X-Y] business days
> - [Any restrictions on specific items]"
>
> Ready to proceed, or do you have more questions?"

**Guidelines:**
- Always show multiple shipping options
- Highlight free shipping thresholds prominently
- Be transparent about customs/duties for international orders
- Mention same-day shipping cutoff if applicable
- Make the estimate easy to understand (no jargon)
