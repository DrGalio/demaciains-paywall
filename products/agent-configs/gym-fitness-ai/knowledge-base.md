### Getting Started

**Q: What should I bring on my first visit?**
A: Workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. Lockers are available — bring your own lock or purchase one at the front desk. We provide complimentary toiletries in the locker rooms.

**Q: Do you offer a free trial?**

**Q: How do I sign up for a membership?**
A: You can sign up at the front desk, on our website at [URL], or through our member app. Bring a valid ID and a form of payment. The process takes about 10-15 minutes.

**Q: Can I tour the gym before joining?**
A: Absolutely! Walk in anytime during staffed hours and we'll give you a guided tour. You can also schedule a tour by calling [phone] or booking online.

### Classes

**Q: Do I need to sign up for classes in advance?**
A: We recommend signing up [X] hours in advance through our member app or at the front desk. Drop-ins are welcome if space is available, but popular classes fill up quickly.

**Q: I've never done [class type] before. Can I still join?**
A: Yes! Most of our classes are designed to accommodate all fitness levels. Our instructors offer modifications throughout. If a class is labeled "Advanced," we recommend trying a beginner-friendly version first.

**Q: What if I sign up for a class but can't make it?**

**Q: Are classes included in my membership?**
A: It depends on your membership tier. Basic members can add a class pack ($[XX] for [X] classes) or upgrade to Premium for unlimited classes. Premium and VIP members have unlimited access.

### Personal Training

**Q: How much does personal training cost?**
A: Single sessions start at $[XX]. We offer packages of 4, 8, 12, and 24 sessions at discounted rates. All new members get a complimentary fitness assessment — ask at the front desk.

**Q: Do I need personal training?**
A: It depends on your goals and experience. PT is great for beginners who want to learn proper form, anyone with specific goals (weight loss, muscle building, sport performance), or people who want accountability. Our trainers can help you decide after a free assessment.

**Q: Can I switch trainers?**
A: Absolutely. We want you to work with someone who fits your style and goals. Just let the front desk know and we'll help you find a better match.

**Q: I have a medical condition. Can I still work out?**
A: In most cases, yes — but we strongly recommend getting clearance from your doctor first. Our trainers are experienced in working with members who have diabetes, high blood pressure, joint issues, and other conditions. Let us know about any limitations so we can help you train safely.

**Q: What should I do if I get injured at the gym?**
A: Notify staff immediately. Our team is trained in first aid and we have first aid kits at the front desk and in each studio. For serious injuries, call 911. After any injury, we recommend seeing a healthcare provider.
