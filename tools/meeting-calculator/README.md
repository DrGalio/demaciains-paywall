# 💸 MeetingCost

**How much money is this meeting burning?**

Shows the real cost of meetings with a live timer that counts money burning in real-time.

## Features

- Calculate meeting cost by attendees, salary, and duration
- Live meeting timer with $/second accumulation
- Weekly and annual cost projections
- "What else could this buy?" comparison
- 100% client-side

## Why This Goes Viral

Everyone hates unnecessary meetings. This tool gives them ammunition:
- "This meeting is costing us $840"
- "We spend $43,680/year on this weekly standup"
- Shareable results that managers can't ignore
