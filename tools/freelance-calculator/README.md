# 💰 FreelanceRate

**Calculate Your REAL Hourly Rate — Free Tool**

Most freelancers undercharge because they calculate their rate wrong. They forget taxes, expenses, non-billable hours, and vacation time.

FreelanceRate shows you what you ACTUALLY need to charge to hit your income goals.

## Features

- Tax-adjusted income calculation
- Business expense inclusion
- Real billable hours (not 40h/week fantasy)
- Minimum, Target, and Premium rate tiers
- Project pricing guide (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Copy-to-clipboard results
- 100% client-side
